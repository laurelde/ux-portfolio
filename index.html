About Me

Hi! I am a User Experience Designer and Front End Developer with a passion for connecting companies to their customers through technology.

I discovered user experience design in college, where my studies combined technology, business, and psychology to effectively research, design, and develop interactive products, and connect businesses with their intended users. Usability testing, design theory, and iterative development help ensure that my prototypes function properly.

Outside of work, I am an avid adventurer and love to travel. I spent four months studying in Denmark, where I lived with an outdoor living & learning community. Some of my favorite moments included getting a backstage tour of the EU Parliament in Brussels, paragliding through Switzerland, and kayaking through the Danish canals.

I'm also an active member of my community in my free time - I work as a producer and lighting specialist on the tech team at my church where I help organize and execute live productions. I also volunteer at a local elementary school on a weekly basis, where I create fun and engaging activities to strengthen the kids' reading and writing comprehension skills.

I'm currently looking for full-time job opportunities involving UX design, user research, or front-end development. Email me at laurelde@umich.edu if you'd like to chat, or connect with me using my social media links below!
